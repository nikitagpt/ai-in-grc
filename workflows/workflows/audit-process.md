# Audit Process Workflow

## Steps
1. Identify controls in scope
2. Create Jira tickets for evidence
3. Collect evidence from teams
4. Upload to auditor portal
5. Address gaps

## Challenges
- Heavy reliance on other teams
- Manual tracking
- Lack of real-time visibility

## Opportunities
- Automate ticket creation
- Centralize evidence tracking
- Use AI to summarize gaps
